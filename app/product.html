Our liquer

LUACO Coffee Cream Liqueur is our most innovative product — and is the first liqueur in the world to feature the rich intensity of wild civet coffee(kopi luwak), combined with superior quality cream. It took two years to perfect the flavour, and over one hundred attempts to ensure its unsurpassable quality.

The drink has subtle tones of chocolate and caramel, which are enhanced when served chilled over ice. The remarkable taste, combined with the natural aphrodisiac effect, make this a decadent after-dinner drink. Enjoy it chilled over ice.

THE SECRET
OF THE WILD
CIVET COFFEE

LuxurybyNature

Wild Civet Coffee (Kopi Luwak) was first discovered in the wilds of Java and Sumatra. The coffee berries are eaten by the civet — and whilst the pulp of the berry is digested, the bean itself passes through the digestive system. The unique mix of enzymes in the civet’s stomach has a remarkable effect on the bean, breaking down bitterness and leaving only rich smoothness behind. Our expert coffee makers hand-select the civet coffee beans from the highlands of the Phillippines, dry them in the sunlight, then lightly roast to release the flavour.
